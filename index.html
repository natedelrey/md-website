Joining

‚úÖ How to Join the Medical Division

Requirements, application links, and bonus rewards.

Basic Requirements

  • Must be in the main Roblox SCPF community and follow all global rules.
  • Must be in the SCPF Medical Division Roblox group.
  • Professional and mature behavior on-site and in Discord.
  • Ability to follow instructions from Medical Division Management and higher ranks.
  • Willingness to meet the current weekly quota (30 minutes on-site + 1 MD task).

Low-effort, troll, or copied applications will be denied. Show effort, detail, and actual interest in the Division.

Application Methods

  1. Google Form: Fill out the official application form: Click here.
  2. AI Interviews: In the Discord, go to #ai-interviews and use /apply for instant, automated interview processing and faster entry.
  3. Wait for a response from staff via Discord and/or Roblox ranking.

üéÅ 200 Robux Bonus for Nurses

We offer a 200 Robux bonus to dedicated members who:

  • Reach the rank of Nurse in the Medical Division.
  • Remain in the Division for at least 2 weeks after achieving Nurse.
  • Have no strikes on record during that time.
  • Are in the payout group: SCPF Outreach Program at the time of payout.

Make sure you join the payout group early so there are no delays when you qualify.

Core Guidelines

  • Professionalism: No trolling, abuse of tools, or intentional disruption on-site or in Discord.
  • Respect: Treat all players and staff respectfully‚Äîno harassment, slurs, or targeted toxicity.
  • Activity: Meet your weekly quota unless you have an approved LOA or reduced quota.
  • Roleplay Quality: Use consistent, immersive medical/psych roleplay that fits the SCPF setting.

Violations may result in warnings, strikes, suspension, or removal depending on severity and history.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the Division.

How long do applications take?

This depends on staff activity and how you applied. /apply in #ai-interviews is immediate results. If you use the Google Form please allow up to [2 days] and avoid spamming staff about your application.

My rank didn‚Äôt update after acceptance.

Contact Management with proof (e.g. acceptance screenshot) so we can update your rank in the Roblox group.

What if I can‚Äôt meet quota?

Real life comes first. Contact Management to request LOA or a temporary reduced quota instead of just disappearing. Communication matters more than perfection.

Can I be in other divisions too?

Yes, you can be in multiple sub-divisons within the Medical Department but each may have seperater activity requirements.

Do I have to use voice chat?

No. However, management may use VC for trainings to make it easier.

Who do I talk to if I have an issue?

Contact a member of Medical Division Management. Include details and screenshots so we can actually help.
